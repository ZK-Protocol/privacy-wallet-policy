Privacy Wallet Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 25, 2025

1. Overview

Privacy Wallet ("we", "our", "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Privacy Wallet Chrome extension.

2. Information Collection

Privacy Wallet is a privacy-focused Ethereum wallet extension. We:

  • Do not collect personal information: We do not collect your name, email address, or other personal identifiers
  • Local storage only: Your private keys, seed phrases, and wallet data are stored entirely on your local device
  • Network requests: We only make network requests for blockchain interaction to the following services:
    • Sepolia testnet (rpc.sepolia.org)
    • Base Sepolia testnet (sepolia.base.org)
    • Base mainnet (mainnet.base.org)
    • Infura node services
    • PublicNode services

3. Data Usage

The limited technical data we process is used only to:

  • Ensure proper wallet functionality
  • Interact with blockchain networks
  • Maintain extension performance and stability

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local storage: All sensitive data (private keys, seed phrases) is stored only in your browser's local storage
  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted locally
  • No server transmission: We do not transmit your private keys or seed phrases to any servers

5. Third-Party Services

Privacy Wallet needs to connect to blockchain networks to execute transactions. Third-party services we use include:

  • Ethereum RPC node services (for blockchain interaction)
  • These services have their own privacy policies, and we recommend reviewing them

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Uninstall the extension at any time
  • Clear all locally stored data
  • Control access to your private keys and wallet data

7. Data Retention

Since we do not store user data on servers, all data retention is controlled by the user. When you uninstall the extension, all local data will be deleted.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through extension updates. Continued use of Privacy Wallet constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
